0x0

Escape Mode Transmit Request

This signal together with txlpdtesc_0, txulpsesc_0, and txtriggeresc[3:0] is used to request the entry into any Escape mode. Once in Escape mode, the lane stays in Escape mode until txrequestesc_0 is de-asserted.
